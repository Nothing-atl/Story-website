The Girl Who Cried When I Disappeared for One Day

November 14, 2025

  I never thought one tiny decision could shake an entire universe.

  It all started when I uninstalled WhatsApp for just one day. One single day. No status, no DP, no last seen..nothing. A clean disappearance.

  Within hours, her world turned upside down.

  First, she checked my chat. “Last seen… gone??”
Then she checked my profile. “DP… gone??”
Then she refreshed. Again. And again. And again.
  By evening, she was pacing around like a detective solving a murder case.

   “where is my Aleena!” she asked everyone.

   She messaged my friends.
She checked mutual groups.
She even asked random people, “Did she block me? Did she delete me? Did aliens kidnap her?!”

  “I think she left the whole world… she didn’t even tell ME. I’m going to cry.”

   For a whole day she searched, waited, refreshed, rechecked… hoping my online bubble would appear again.

   And when I finally came back the next morning cool, calm, and casual.. she literally burst out:

   “WHERE WERE YOU?! DO YOU KNOW HOW MANY PEOPLE I ASKED TO FIND YOU?!”
Her voice shaking. Her eyes watery.
Her heart? Completely dramatic.
  I couldn’t help but laugh, but deep down it felt nice.

   Because in that one day…
someone cared enough to miss me, worry for me, and search for me like I was the last cookie in the jar.
   And that girl?
She’ll probably never let me uninstall anything again.
